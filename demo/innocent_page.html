By Alex Johnson • June 10, 2026 • 5 min read

In today's fast-paced world, staying productive is more important than ever. Here are our top tips to boost your efficiency.

1 Time Blocking Method

Divide your day into focused blocks of 90 minutes. Research shows this aligns with your brain's natural ultradian rhythm, maximizing deep work capacity.

2 The Two-Minute Rule

If a task takes less than two minutes, do it immediately. This prevents small tasks from piling up and consuming mental bandwidth.

3 Digital Detox Hours

Designate at least 2 hours per day completely screen-free. Read a physical book, take a walk, or practice mindfulness meditation.

4 The Eisenhower Matrix

Categorize tasks into four quadrants: Urgent+Important, Important+Not Urgent, Urgent+Not Important, and Neither. Focus on quadrant 2 for long-term success.
